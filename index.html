About Us

AVENTOS PARTNERS invests into innovative business models and strong entrepreneurs. We provide hands-on coverage and a strong network to improve and advance our portfolio companies. We aim to invest into a concentrated portfolio of businesses alongside strong partners.

Investment Strategy

INNOVATIVE BUSINESS MODELS
We focus on innovative, technology-based business models with a clear value proposition and long-term strategy.
EARLY STAGE
We invest into early stage companies. We are looking for business models with a proof of concept, first prototypes of their product and first customer relations. Ticket sizes range between 50–100k EUR.
CONCENTRATED PORTFOLIO
We invest into a small number of businesses to ensure our resource support the development and growth of our portfolio companies. Our regional focus is Germany.
PRAGMATIC ENTREPRENEURS
We are investing into people. We are looking for a committed, pragmatic and complementary team of entrepreneurs.
STRONG PARTNERS
We aim to invest alongside other professional seed and venture capital investors.

What do we Offer

SMART MONEY — We act as sparring partner and counsel in various fields such as legal, IT and commercial.

NETWORK — We actively support our portfolio companies in search for capital and business partners.

LEAN STRUCTURE — Coverage and structure of investment designed to fit the needs of our portfolio companies.

Team

AVENTOS PARTNERS is a team of pragmatic and fast decision-making investors. We are predominantly active in top-consultancies, venture capital and private equity funds, law firms as well as successful startups. The major part of us has already founded own businesses or invested seed money.
