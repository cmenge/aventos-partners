Smart Money

Investment Focus

We invest into innovative business models and strong entrepreneurs. Our investment focus is not restricted to internet-based business models but also includes innovative concepts within established industries.

We are looking for entrepreneurs with a proven concept and a highly committed team. We intend to invest alongside professional co-investors, such as business angels and VC funds.

In summary we are looking for the following targets:

  • seed-stage & early-stage VC
  • innovative business models in "old and new" economies
  • proof-of-concept
  • strong and committed management team
  • co-investing with other investors

TEAM

We are a group of young, pragmatic and fast decision-making investors. We are predominantly active in top-consultancies, venture capital and private equity funds, law firms as well as successful startups. The majority of us has already founded own businesses or invested seed money.

WHAT DO WE OFFER

SMART MONEY – Given our complementary set of qualifications and experiences we do not only provide capital to our investments, but also act as a sparring partner and counsel in various fields such as legal, IT and commercial.

STRUCTURAL SETUP – We are investing our own money and can thus come to decisions extremely swiftly. During the due diligence process and after a potential investment target companies will have dedicated business partners within our group to keep the investment process as lean as possible and reduce complexity.

CO-INVESTORS – We aim to invest alongside other professional investors. We are happy to actively support the search for further investors within our networks.
